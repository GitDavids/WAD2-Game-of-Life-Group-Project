About

This app is meant to share interesting sessions on Conwayâ€™s game of life. This is a game played without any active players, the only thing that is required is an initial state of 0s and 1s (or black and white spots on a grid). The state then continuously evolves based on the initial state, sometimes creating unique and mesmerising patterns.

This application is to give people who are interested in this game the opportunity to share and see cool design evolutions.

To find out more about the game logic, head to the Game Logic page.
